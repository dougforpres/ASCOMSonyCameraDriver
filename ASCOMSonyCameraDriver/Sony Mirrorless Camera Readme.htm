Sony Mirrorless ASCOM Camera Driver

This is an ASCOM driver for Sony Mirrorless cameras. The driver should work with any Sony camera that connects to the Sony Imaging Edge Remote application in USB mode.

This driver was developed on Windows 10, with a Sony a6400 camera.

I'd like to thank all of the people who have helped test the driver, and who have contributed their camera profiles so that the driver will work with more models.

ASCOM Initiative

The ASCOM Initiative consists of a group of astronomy software developers and instrument vendors whose goals are to promote the driver/client model and scripting automation.
